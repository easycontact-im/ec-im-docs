### Route to the Right Team

Use alert rules and escalation routing to ensure incidents reach the appropriate team based on severity, service, environment, or custom tags.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Alert Rules">
    Filter, transform, and control incoming alerts before they become incidents.
    - Suppress noisy alerts from development environments
    - Normalize severity levels across different tools
    - Add tags for routing and categorization
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Escalation Routing">
    Route incidents to specific escalation policies based on alert attributes.
    - Match on severity, host, service, environment
    - Use custom tags for customer-specific routing
    - Support 13 different condition operators
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Build On-Call Schedules

Create rotation schedules to ensure 24/7 coverage with automatic handoffs.

- **Daily or weekly rotations** with configurable handoff times
- **Multiple layers** for primary, secondary, and specialized coverage
- **Time restrictions** for business hours, nights, or weekends
- **Temporary overrides** for vacations and shift swaps

### Escalate Until Acknowledged

Define multi-level escalation policies that notify the right people in sequence until someone responds.

- **Multiple escalation levels** with configurable delays
- **Parallel or sequential** target notification
- **Multiple channels**: Call, SMS, Email, Push, Webhooks
- **Automatic retries** for missed calls
- **Repeat cycles** if no one responds

### Notify via Any Channel

Reach your team through their preferred channels with smart notifications.

| Channel | Use Case |
|---------|----------|
| **Voice Call** | Critical alerts requiring immediate attention |
| **SMS** | Quick notifications when on the go |
| **Email** | Detailed alerts with full context |
| **Push** | Mobile app notifications |
| **Webhooks** | Slack, Teams, Discord, and custom integrations |
